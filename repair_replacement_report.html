Water damage is a small, steady slice — not what's driving the camera trend. Confirmed water-ingress bots ran 2–3/month (Feb–Apr) and fell to 0 in Jun–Jul. The big May spike (37 tickets) was overwhelmingly a genuine camera-hardware cohort — "black camera"/IR-failure and FPGA/crosslink faults (22 confirmed camera faults, only 1 water). All 8 water bots were logged as "Returning for repair" in HubSpot but were actually scrapped & replaced per Slack — a tagging gap worth closing.
